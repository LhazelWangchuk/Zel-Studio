About Us

Hey there! I’m a passionate student at GCIT in Thimphu, Bhutan, chasing my dream of becoming a graphic designer.

At Zel Studio, I channel my love for bold, vibrant designs into creating graphic posters that tell a story. Being based in the heart of Bhutan, I draw inspiration from our rich cultural heritage, blending traditional elements with modern aesthetics. I’m always experimenting with new ideas, playing with colors, and pushing the boundaries of creativity to craft designs that not only look good but also connect with people on a deeper level. Let’s create something amazing together!
